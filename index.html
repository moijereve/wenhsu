WEN HSU

Wen Hsu is a film curator, producer and critic. He is a Berlinale Talents alumni, working as a film international sales for many years. He was on the competition jurors for many film festivals including Women Make Waves(Taiwan), South Taiwan and Kaohsiung Film Festival. He founded a film production/curatorial collective "Ge·stell" in Taipei which has a special focus on moving image, artist's film and experimental cinema, their first short film production “SAPPUKEI” won the Flash Competition Prize at FIDMarseille.
